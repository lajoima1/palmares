name: Broghies nachos
description: Low carb and gd-friendly snack to satisfy a pizza or nacho craving.
servings: 8-10 broghies
prep_time: 5 minutes
cook_time: 10 minutes
total_time: 15 minutes
difficulty: Easy
tags: snack, gd-friendly

ingredients:
- Broghies
- Salsa or tomato sauce (low sugar)
- Shredded cheese

instructions:
1. Add thin layer of sauce to broghies.
2. Spread some cheese on top.
3. Cook in mini-oven at 400 until cheese is melted.

notes:
- Fantastic snack for gestational diabetes.
